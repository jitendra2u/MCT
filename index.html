Who is Medyhne ?

My name is Medyhne Lebachen and my goal is to have a abundant and joy-filled existence, doing what I love and helping you to do the same! Interestingly, the root of the name Medyhne -Medha in Sanskrit, means Divine Feminine Sacred Power (I’ll take that lol!)

On my journey I have completed two tertiary qualifications exploring Psychology, Sociology, people and our amazing world! I was then inspired by fitness and became a qualified Aerobics Instructor, achieving super fitness levels! I married, became a mum of three beautiful kids whom I love dearly (and a fourth baby girl who died at birth… we don’t talk often enough about death as being a part of life!) and I am blessed to have come from a lovely family (mum, dad, step-mum- who to their immense credit are all close, and two sisters).

As every human being does, I have experienced my fair share of trials, tribulations and heartache, such as deaths, divorce and so forth, but I always forged on with an optimistic and pioneering spirit full of hope for the future and can now teach/guide/inspire/inform and pass on some valuable skills I learnt along the way!

So then, in the scheme of life, my fascination turned to spiritual growth. I trained in numerous Holistic Modalities like Kiniergetics, became a Reiki Master, gained an American qualification as a ‘Minister of Healing’ and other Healing modalities.

I have worked extensively in Life Coaching, Healing, Holistic Health, Personal Growth & Development fields (with adults and children) in Schools, Health Practices, Community Centers and Commercial locations & Healing Centres. This included being invited as a Speaker & Contributor at a Victorian Student Representative Group Summit and taking classes at the prestigious “The Peninsula School” Mt Eliza for final year students in ‘Life Skills and Relaxation' (Stress-Busting Skills!)

Along the way I have travelled extensively overseas and in Australia including living in a caravan for a while, like a gypsy. I have explored creative interests like pottery, embroidery, doll & rug making, weaving and photography in which I received a place in a tertiary photography competition!

Creatively, I have written and performed songs seemingly forever(!), singing solo on national television, scoring an International Song Publishing Deal in 10 countries, having a song (with Graham Oxford from our band Sonic Remedy) in a US movie, recording and selling my CD’s, recording my song with a beautiful School Choir, and having an International film company shoot a video for my song (virtually the only sad one in my collection lol!!) “Sun Don’t Shine Today”.

I loved presenting my two popular community radio shows (including co-hosting a live Anzac Day Parade in Cranbourne of over 9,000 people & interviewing number one Australian country music artist for many years, Graeme Connors.

In the writing department (no pun intended!), I am a relatively prolific Published author, with six books being released within the next year. My book “Hilhairyass Poems” is a fun collection of 41 poems about all aspects of life aimed at 9 year olds and upwards. There is a 10 track playful CD available that enhances 10 of the poems, sold separately. The illustrations are by top Australian illustrator & artist Mark Payne (who was illustrator in the number one Penguin series of books “Aussie Bites”) and I chortled and chatted with radio and print media, up the Eastern border of Australia, in a promotional tour, when it was released. Articles of mine have been published also in National magazines like “Living Now”.

For many years I have sold paintings and have illustrated my book “GODDESS Maidens Of Medyhne”. My prints are now available here online. As are the stunning prints by Rosa, a Taiwanese artist.

On the personal level, I have had grand love in my life, beautiful friendships and met some great people! I have tried at all times to embody the virtues of compassion and kindness (while being human at the same time and working on my own growth lol!) with the intention of leaving a positive and helpful footprint, on the Earth we share.

I love animals and brought home a duck, a tortoise and some stray dogs when I was a child (among other things!).

Horse riding was a passion and I was unjustifiably proud to receive a third placing in a gymkhana when all, bar two, of the entrants bolted from the ring! I have swum with dolphins, held two baby lion cubs (at once!) and wild pythons in Byron Bay, had a migration of blue spotted butterflies flying overhead for hours, and unforgettably, been surrounded by a pod of killer whales off the Bay Of Islands, New Zealand, while on a boat trip. I have eaten the world’s best ice cream (arguably!) in Florence, climbed Mt Sinai (dodging tear gas!), seen Pompei, the Statue Of David and Tutankamen’s Tomb. I danced in ‘The Sound of Music” Gazebo in Vienna, Austria, had a lead role in an Amateur Theatre production, scored a bit-part speaking role in Vince Colosimo’s first movie, and was nanny to a poodle in Paris. Heck, a Shiekh in Egypt even offered to buy me for quite a few camels (gulp…my response was “No thanks!”)

I guess you could say my life has not been boring and the night is still young. As far as I can tell…I haven’t even started yet!!! But enough about me… let’s talk about you? How would you like your dream life to be?

Let me help you explore the wonder and the unfolding masterpiece that is YOUR life… Let me help you through my books, classes and coaching, achieve your ultimate potential in this lifetime. Don’t pass from this Earthly experience with your heartfelt desires for a meaningful life still locked inside you!

Sometimes it feels impossible to overcome our own self imposed blockages to achieve these breakthroughs alone! We all need support. After all, as the saying goes, “No man is an island”. What is your unique contribution to this world to make your life a life of NO regrets!

What has stopped you from breaking through in the past?

At My Creative Therapies… I CAN HELP !

Let’s join together to make your life everything you have ever dreamed!!!

I will do everything within my power to support this grand vision of your ‘best life’…

the life you were born to live!

Much love
Medyhne
